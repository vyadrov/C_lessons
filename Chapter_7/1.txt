Over the next seven hours or so, Trump took aim at everything and anyone he could, unleashing a barrage of more than 30 tweets and retweets that targeted media outlets, high profile commentators and hosts, and Democrats.
He also returned once more to the Russia probe and impeachment, promoting a tweet that accused his political adversaries of three failed coup attempts. The tweet went on to suggest with no evidence that the presidents opponents could attempt to steal the election by making the novel coronavirus impact on human lives seem worse than it really is.
